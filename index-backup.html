Hello, I'm Fatimah ✨

Obsessed with cats, in love with coffee

Caffeinated Cats started with a dream — one day, a full cat café where you can sip a perfect latte with a purring friend by your side. Until then, we're bringing that warm, cozy café experience straight to your events.

Whether it's your wedding, a baby shower, a corporate gathering, or a community pop-up, we show up with quality drinks, good vibes, and plenty of cat-themed love.
